Home Security Tips

Ensure the safety and security of your home with these tips:

  • Install deadbolts on all exterior doors.
  • Use window locks and secure sliding doors.
  • Keep your home well-lit with motion-sensor lights.
  • Install a security system and monitor it regularly.
  • Never share spare keys with others. Use a lockbox instead.

Housing Resources

Access resources for finding and maintaining housing:

  • U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) - Information on housing assistance and programs.
  • Zillow - Search for rental properties and homes for sale.
  • Apartment Finder - Find rental apartments and housing options.
  • Rent.com - Search for apartments, homes, and rental properties.

Safety Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure your personal safety:

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid distractions while walking.
  • Lock your car and keep valuables out of sight.
  • Know your neighbors and stay informed about local safety issues.
  • Have an emergency plan and share it with family members.
  • Report any suspicious activity to local authorities.
